1. Wholesale Distribution (Brydens Antigua)

Pain Points

  • Inventory management — Manual stock tracking, overstock/understock issues
  • Order processing — Phone/fax orders, data entry errors
  • Customer service — Repeated inquiries about stock, pricing, delivery
  • Accounts receivable — Chasing payments, invoice follow-ups
  • Route optimization — Inefficient delivery scheduling

Automation Solutions

  • Inventory Management: Real-time tracking, automated reorder alerts, demand forecasting (ROI: 40% reduction in stockouts)
  • Order Processing: Online portal, automated fulfillment, accounting integration (ROI: 60% faster processing)
  • Customer Service AI: 24/7 chatbot for inquiries (ROI: 50% fewer service calls)
  • Automated Invoicing: Instant generation, payment reminders, late fees (ROI: 35% faster collection)
  • Route Optimization: AI-powered planning, real-time tracking (ROI: 20% fuel savings)
